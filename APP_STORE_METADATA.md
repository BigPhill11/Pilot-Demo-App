## Description (max 4000 chars — paste this into App Store Connect)

Phil's Financials is your personal money mentor, teaching real-world financial skills through interactive lessons, flashcards, and quizzes — guided by Phil, a friendly panda who makes finance approachable and fun.

Whether you're just starting your financial journey or looking to sharpen your money skills, Phil's Financials covers everything from your first paycheck to long-term wealth building.

**WHAT YOU'LL LEARN**

💰 Income & Career
- How to increase your hourly value, not just your hours
- Negotiation strategies that actually work
- Avoiding burnout while growing your earning power

📊 Financial Planning
- Setting goals with clear time horizons
- Separating wants from needs
- Building a priority stack for your money

🏦 Saving & Emergency Funds
- Why saving comes before spending
- How to build and maintain an emergency fund
- Automating savings so you never forget

📈 Investing
- What you're actually buying when you invest
- Understanding risk, volatility, and price movement
- Diversification and long-term strategy

💳 Credit & Debt
- Understanding your credit score
- The difference between good and bad debt
- Strategies for paying down debt fast

🛡️ Insurance & Protection
- Protecting your assets and income
- Fraud, scams, and identity protection
- Legal basics every adult should know

🧾 Taxes
- How income types affect your tax bill
- Deductions, credits, and tax-advantaged accounts
- Tax planning mindset for long-term wealth

**HOW IT WORKS**

Each topic is broken into short, focused lessons you can complete in 5–15 minutes. After each lesson, test your knowledge with quizzes and reinforce concepts with swipeable flashcards. Follow Phil through comic-style panels that make complex ideas click.

**DESIGNED FOR REAL LIFE**

No jargon. No fluff. Just the financial concepts you actually need — explained the way a smart friend would explain them, with real examples and practical takeaways.

Start free. Learn at your own pace. Build the financial confidence you deserve.

## INSTRUCTIONS FOR SUBMISSION

### Step 1: Archive in Xcode
1. Xcode is already open with the App.xcworkspace
2. In the toolbar at top, set the scheme to **App** and destination to **Any iOS Device (arm64)**
3. Go to **Product → Archive** in the menu bar
4. Wait 5–10 minutes for the archive to complete
5. The Organizer window will open automatically

### Step 2: Upload to App Store Connect
1. In Organizer, select the PhilsFinancials archive
2. Click **Distribute App**
3. Select **App Store Connect** → **Upload**
4. Follow the prompts (Automatic signing should work with your Apple Developer account)
5. Enter your Apple ID (phillipghead@gmail.com) and 2FA code when prompted

### Step 3: Complete the submission
1. Log in to https://appstoreconnect.apple.com
2. Select Phil's Financials → iOS App → 1.0 Prepare for Submission
3. Fill in the metadata from this file
4. Upload screenshots
5. Click **Add for Review** → **Submit to App Review**

### Alternative if Xcode GUI doesn't work:
1. You need to **free up 10+ GB of disk space** first
2. Then run: `npm run build && npx cap sync ios`
3. Then: `xcodebuild archive` (see build/PhilsFinancials.xcarchive output)
